# Screenshot Templates

Capture screenshots from production-like builds with realistic local data and no debug overlays.

## App Store

- 6.5" iPhone screenshots for the primary phone listing.
- 12.9" iPad screenshots for the tablet listing.
- Include Garden, Calendar, Discover, Plant Detail, Journal, Daily Flower, Scan, and Profile.
- Provide light and dark mode variants when possible.
- Keep status bar, locale, and sample plant data consistent across the set.

## Play Store

- Phone screenshots in portrait.
- Tablet screenshots in portrait or landscape when the layout benefits from the wider viewport.
- Include the same golden-path surfaces used for App Store screenshots.
- Avoid emulator chrome, debug banners, placeholder-only gardens, or missing image states.
