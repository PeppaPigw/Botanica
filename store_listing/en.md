# Botanica

## Subtitle
Calm, intelligent plant care

## Description
Botanica helps you care for every plant with a quiet daily rhythm: a clear garden view, timely care tasks, growth notes, and a journal made for the small changes that matter.

Weather-adapted recommendations bring local temperature and humidity into your care routine, so watering, light, and seasonal adjustments feel less like guesswork. Add plants from the library, follow upcoming tasks, and keep each plant's story in one place.

Daily flower rituals and optional AI notes add a gentle layer of reflection without overwhelming the practical work of plant care. Botanica is designed to feel premium, focused, and easy to return to every day.
